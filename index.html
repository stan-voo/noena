FAQs

Does it hurt? ⌄
Most clients feel brief warmth; the cooling tip keeps it comfortable.
Is it safe for my skin tone? ⌄
We assess your Fitzpatrick type and adjust energy settings before any session.
How many sessions? ⌄
Typically 6–8 for underarms; varies by hair growth and hormones.
Can I do it if I wax? ⌄
Yes—switch to shaving during the course for best results.
Any contraindications? ⌄
We screen medications, pregnancy, infections, and recent sun exposure first.
